Professional Email Example

Subject: Question Regarding Upcoming Marketing Project - [Your Name]


Dear Ms. Vance,


I hope this email finds you well.

I am writing to inquire about the next steps for the upcoming marketing project we discussed during our meeting on Tuesday. Specifically, I would appreciate clarification on the preferred format for submitting our initial proposal and the deadline for this submission.

I have started outlining the preliminary ideas and want to ensure I am on the right track. Please let me know if there's a specific template or additional resources I should review.


Thank you for your guidance.


Best regards,
[Student Name]
Marketing Intern
